How to buy from us

For direct sales, ring or email us your enquiry or order. See the Contact Us page for details.

We post to you, or you can collect from us by prior arrangement.

We are located in Parkdale, Melbourne.

Payment

Our invoice will be included in your posted order and will allow for direct deposit payment. Cash on pickup is also available.

Payment by cheque or postal order is available, and goods can be sent worldwide. (Payment by cheque is unavailable for overseas orders, we are based in Australia).

Sorry but we do not accept payment by card.
