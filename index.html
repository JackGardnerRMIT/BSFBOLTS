Who Are We?

We supply fasteners and related tooling for the repair or restoration of:

  • Vintage and Classic Cars
  • Vintage Motorcycles
  • Vintage Aircraft
  • Old and rare machinery
  • Model Engineering Projects

We can supply fasteners in these thread types:

BSF (British Standard Fine)

BSB (British Standard Brass)

BSW (British Standard Whitworth)

UNF (Unified National Fine)

UNC (Unified National Coarse)

Tooling

Our Stock

Below is a general idea of our stock – not all sizes or combinations are available.

If you would like a copy of our catolog, please contact us and we can email it to you.

BSF

BSF bolts, setscrews, screws, nuts, locknuts, with head types including hex, cheesehead, countersunk, roundhead, mushroom head, with slotted, Philips and Allen drives, in a variety of materials, tensile strengths, and plating types, with diameters ranging from 3/16” to 1 ¼”, and lengths ranging from ¼” to 6”. We also have a range of engineering taps and dies, including some rare left hand thread.

BA

Bolts and screws in a range of head types, and nuts, from size 0BA to size 12BA, as well as engineering taps and dies to size 12.

BSW

We stock a range of Whitworth fasteners in diameters from 1/8" to 1 1/4".

Other Items

We also have some items in brass thread (BSB - 26 TPI), cycle thread (BSCY - 20 and 26 TPI), UNF, UNC, as well as a range of flat and spring washers, and some miscellaneous hardware such as clevis pins and split pins. Many of our items are from military or aircraft sources, and some have a NSN code (NATO Stock Number) for exact identification.

We also now stock a large range of slotted wood screws in brass and steel, in various gauges and lengths - please enquire for details.
